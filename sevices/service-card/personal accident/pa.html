What’s Personal Accident Insurance?

Whether it's a freak mishap or just bad luck, this insurance helps with medical bills, daily expenses, and even some payouts to your family if things go south.

Understanding Your Personal Protection Policy Coverage

Your Policy Includes Coverage For:

  • Accidental Death: Compensation will be provided to the designated beneficiary in the event of accidental death occurring during the policy term.
  • Permanent Total Disablement: Coverage for the total and permanent loss of limbs, vision, etc., as a result of an accident.
  • Accidental claim Expense Reimbursement: Reimbursement for expenses incurred during claim for a minimum of 24 hours due to an injury sustained within 7 days of the accident.
  • Accidental Hospital Daily Allowance: A daily allowance for claim exceeding 30 days following the insured event, with a one-day deductible.

Personal Accident Insurance Benefits:

Accidental Death Benefit:

Provided to the nominated beneficiary in the event of the insured's death due to an accident. In cases of total permanent disability, the insurer will pay 100% of the sum insured if the policyholder is permanently incapacitated and unable to earn income for life.

Accidental claim Expense Reimbursement: If the policyholder requires claim due to an accident during the policy period, the insurer will cover the claim costs.
